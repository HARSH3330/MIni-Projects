## BIRTHDAY POLICY

SIGNIFY INNOVATIONS INDIA LTD.,

SIGNIFY INNOVATION LABS &amp; SIGNIFY COOPER LIGHTING INDIA

<!-- image -->

## 1. Objective

To encourage Company's involvement in the personal events of employees through Line Managers.

## 2. Eligibility

All Signify India employees (H9 &amp; above) are eligible under this policy.

## 3. Entitlement

- · The Line manager can choose to celebrate a team member's birthday with the department/team.
- · Expenses can be reimbursed upto a maximum of INR 1000 per event.
- · All such expenses can be reimbursed through concur. All supporting bills will need to be attached.

The company has the exclusive right to amend or terminate this policy without any prior notice.

Any clarifications related to interpretation of this policy can be referred to CHRO - India and the decision of  CHRO - India in this regard will be final and binding.
